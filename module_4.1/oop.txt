                           oop stands for object-oriented programmig. 
                           procedural  programming is about writing procedural or functions that perform operations on the date, 
                           while object-oriented progamming is about creating objects that contain both data and functions.

                           there are six type of oop concepts :-
                                1. class.
                                2. object.
                                3. inheritance.
                                4. encapsulation.
                                5. abstraction.
                                6. polymorphism.
